27.8 GDB/MI断点命令 ¶

本节记录用于操作断点的GDB/MI命令。

The -break-after命令 ¶

概要 ¶

 -break-after number count

断点编号number在被命中count次之前不会生效。要了解这在‘-break-list’命令的输出中是如何体现的，请参阅下面‘-break-list’命令的描述。

GDB命令 ¶

相应的GDB命令是‘ignore’。

示例 ¶

(gdb)
-break-insert main
^done,bkpt={number="1",type="breakpoint",disp="keep",
enabled="y",addr="0x000100d0",func="main",file="hello.c",
fullname="/home/foo/hello.c",line="5",thread-groups=["i1"],
times="0"}
(gdb)
-break-after 1 3
~
^done
(gdb)
-break-list

^done,BreakpointTable={nr_rows="1",nr_cols="6",
hdr=[{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="number",colhdr="Num"},
{width="14",alignment="-1",col_name="type",colhdr="Type"},
{width="4",alignment="-1",col_name="disp",colhdr="Disp"},
{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="enabled",colhdr="Enb"},
{width="10",alignment="-1",col_name="addr",colhdr="Address"},
{width="40",alignment="2",col_name="what",colhdr="What"}],
body=[bkpt={number="1",type="breakpoint",disp="keep",enabled="y",
addr="0x000100d0",func="main",file="hello.c",fullname="/home/foo/hello.c",
line="5",thread-groups=["i1"],times="0",ignore="3"}]}
(gdb)

The -break-commands命令 ¶

概要 ¶

 -break-commands number [ command1 ... commandN ]

指定在断点number被命中时应执行的CLI命令。参数command1到commandN是命令。如果未指定命令，则清除任何先前设置的命令。请参阅 断点命令列表。此功能的典型用途是跟踪程序，即每当断点被命中时打印一些变量的值，然后继续执行。

GDB命令 ¶

相应的GDB命令是‘commands’。

示例 ¶

(gdb)
-break-insert main
^done,bkpt={number="1",type="breakpoint",disp="keep",
enabled="y",addr="0x000100d0",func="main",file="hello.c",
fullname="/home/foo/hello.c",line="5",thread-groups=["i1"],
times="0"}
(gdb)
-break-commands 1 "print v" "continue"
^done
(gdb)

The -break-condition命令 ¶

概要 ¶

 -break-condition [ --force ] number [ expr ]

仅当expr中的条件为真时，断点number才会使程序停止。该条件成为‘-break-list’输出的一部分（请参阅下面‘-break-list’命令的描述）。如果传递了‘--force’标志，则即使该条件对于断点number的所有位置都无效，也会强制定义该条件。如果省略expr参数，则断点number变为无条件断点。

GDB命令 ¶

相应的GDB命令是‘condition’。

示例 ¶

(gdb)
-break-condition 1 1
^done
(gdb)
-break-list
^done,BreakpointTable={nr_rows="1",nr_cols="6",
hdr=[{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="number",colhdr="Num"},
{width="14",alignment="-1",col_name="type",colhdr="Type"},
{width="4",alignment="-1",col_name="disp",colhdr="Disp"},
{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="enabled",colhdr="Enb"},
{width="10",alignment="-1",col_name="addr",colhdr="Address"},
{width="40",alignment="2",col_name="what",colhdr="What"}],
body=[bkpt={number="1",type="breakpoint",disp="keep",enabled="y",
addr="0x000100d0",func="main",file="hello.c",fullname="/home/foo/hello.c",
line="5",cond="1",thread-groups=["i1"],times="0",ignore="3"}]}
(gdb)

The -break-delete命令 ¶

概要 ¶

 -break-delete ( breakpoint )+

删除参数列表中指定编号的断点。这在断点列表中会明显体现出来。

GDB命令 ¶

相应的GDB命令是‘delete’。

示例 ¶

(gdb)
-break-delete 1
^done
(gdb)
-break-list
^done,BreakpointTable={nr_rows="0",nr_cols="6",
hdr=[{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="number",colhdr="Num"},
{width="14",alignment="-1",col_name="type",colhdr="Type"},
{width="4",alignment="-1",col_name="disp",colhdr="Disp"},
{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="enabled",colhdr="Enb"},
{width="10",alignment="-1",col_name="addr",colhdr="Address"},
{width="40",alignment="2",col_name="what",colhdr="What"}],
body=[]}
(gdb)

The -break-disable命令 ¶

概要 ¶

 -break-disable ( breakpoint )+

禁用指定的breakpoint。在断点列表中，名为breakpoint的断点的‘enabled’字段现在设置为‘n’。

GDB命令 ¶

相应的GDB命令是‘disable’。

示例 ¶

(gdb)
-break-disable 2
^done
(gdb) 
-break-list
^done,BreakpointTable={nr_rows="1",nr_cols="6",
hdr=[{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="number",colhdr="Num"},
{width="14",alignment="-1",col_name="type",colhdr="Type"},
{width="4",alignment="-1",col_name="disp",colhdr="Disp"},
{width="3",alignment="-1",col_name="enabled",colhdr="Enb"},
{width="10",alignment="-1",col_name="addr",colhdr="Address"},
{width="40",alignment="2",col_name="what",colhdr="What"}],
body=[bkpt={number="2",type="breakpoint",disp="keep",enabled="n",
addr="0x000100d0",func="main",file="hello.c",fullname="/home/foo/hello.c",
line="5",thread-groups=["i1"],times="0"}]}
(gdb)
